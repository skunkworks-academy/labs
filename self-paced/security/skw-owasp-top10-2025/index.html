A05

Injection

Simulated query and browser rendering issues.

A06

Insecure Design

Abuse cases and business logic controls.

A07

Authentication Failures

Session tampering and brute-force controls.

A08

Integrity Failures

A09

Logging & Alerting

Log injection, alerting, forensic readiness.

A10

Exceptional Conditions

Verbose errors and fail-open behavior.
